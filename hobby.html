Fulfilling my free time

In this section you can read about my favourite free-time activities

Sport

I am a very energetic person, so sport has always been my way of using that extra energy. When I was a little girl I was taking modern ballet classes. After that I went swimming. And when I was 12 years old, I trained volleyball for 4 years. I played basketball for only one year, but it became my favourite sport. Now I am going to the gym, but I still play basketball every time I get the chance.

Rollerblading

In summer and spring my favourite activity is rollerblading. It is exciting and sometimes full of adrenalin. I regularly go rollerblading with my childhood friend.

Friends

Last but not least, I love hanging out with my friends. They are full of support, kindness and energy. We usually go out for coffee or long walks. After all they are the ones that give the best advices and recommendations for movies, series and books
